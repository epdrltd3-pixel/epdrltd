About EPDR

Electrical Power Development & Restoration Ltd is a Jamaican electrical and solar contracting company focused on reliable execution, professional reporting, and practical solutions for complex electrical environments. We work with commercial properties, hospitality facilities, residential developments, villas, and infrastructure-type projects where safety, uptime, and compliance matter.

Core Services

EPDR delivers electrical and energy solutions for commercial, hospitality, and residential infrastructure projects across Jamaica.

Commercial Electrical Systems

Full electrical installations, upgrades, distribution boards, wiring systems, and facility-wide power solutions for hotels, resorts, and commercial buildings.

Solar PV & Energy Storage

Design and installation of solar systems, hybrid inverters, battery backup solutions, and energy efficiency optimization for commercial and residential clients.

Electrical Maintenance & Fault Finding

Diagnostic testing, troubleshooting, power restoration, load balancing, and preventative maintenance for critical systems.

Panel Inspection & Remediation

Thermal inspection, tightening, cleaning, compliance checks, and corrective electrical panel work to improve safety and reliability.

Lightning Protection & Earthing

Inspection and restoration of grounding systems, lightning protection networks, and surge protection systems for facilities.

Technical Reporting

Formal inspection reports, electrical assessments, compliance documentation, and project handover documentation for clients and engineers.
